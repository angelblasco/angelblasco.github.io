What do I do?

I am currently a PhD student in UNED under the supervision of Victor Manuel Jimenez Morales. We are studying Multisymplectic Geometry, a branch of Differential Geometry, and its applications to Classical Field Theories. A list of interesting papers and books to introduce the study of this branch:

Papers

F. Cantrijn, A. Ibort and M. De León, "On the geometry of multisymplectic manifolds". DOI: https://doi.org/10.1017/S1446788700036636.


Books

M. de León and R. Izquierdo-López, "Graded poisson and graded dirac structures", J. Math. Phys. 1 February 2025; 66 (2): 022901. DOI: 10.1063/5.0243128.
